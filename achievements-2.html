Our Awards and Achievements

Our students are exposed to reputable International and National competitions.
The Competition training programme is opened to all students and they are nurtured from Year 7.

Some of these competitions include:

1. INTERNATIONAL MATHEMATICS AND SCIENCE OLYMPIAD
2. AMERICAN MATHEMATICS COMPETITION (AMC)
3. COWBELL NATIONAL SECONDARY SCHOOL MATHEMATICS COMPETITION (NASSMAC)
4. NNPC SCIENCE COMPETITION
5. NNPC/CHEVRON NATIONAL ART COMPETITION
