How to Apply

Admission is strictly by merit

Admission is strictly by merit without bias of sex, religion,
ethnic or cultural background.
Applicants must be at least 10 years of age to be eligible for admission.


The procedure is as follows:

1. Obtain application forms and prospectus from designated sales centres or Apply Online.

2. Fill and return forms with all necessary documents.

3. A highly competitive entrance examination is taken.

4. An interview is conducted for successful candidates and their parents.

5. A letter of offer of admission is given and upon fulfillment of all conditions, admission is confirmed.
