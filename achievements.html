Our Awards and Achievements

Our students are exposed to reputable National competitions.
The Competition training programme is opened to all students and the school picks the best student the represent the school.

Some of these competitions include:

  1. 1. WAZOBIA Radio Debate

  2. 2. WAZOBIA speeling competition 

  3. 3. Reezens education foundation quiz competition

  4. 4. AVOSCO Science Competition

  5. 5. Moral Education Championship

On 27th May 2022, the school was represented by nine (9) students at WAZOBIA Radio debate. Udoh Onyinyechi emerged the best speaker at the competition. The Superior General in admiration gave her one year scholarship and other eight Students one term scholarship in order to motivate them and other students.

On 27th September 2022, the school represented by both junior and senior students did exceptionally well at another competition organized by the same WAZOBIA radio station in Onitsha. This time it was spelling “Bee.” Okoli Amanda took the first position in junior category, while Okeke Oluchi took the first position in senior category.

Meanwhile on 20th October 2021, Jacinta Eworo represented the school in science quiz organized by Reezens education foundation at St. Albert the great catholic parish, Abatete. Miss Jacinta then in senior class five came 3rd and was awarded a one year scholarship by the foundation.

The school had succeeded in producing 122 graduands since 2014 till date. Most of the pupils were admitted into most prestigious secondary schools in the state ranging from Federal Girls College Onitsha, Christ the King College Onitsha, Queen of the Rosary College Onitsha, All Hallow Seminary Onitsha, Francis Cardinal Arinze Secondary School Nkwelle Ezunaka among others. Our pupils have also emerged winners in quizzes and other competitions. One significant one was the AVOSCO Science Competition in 2019 in which we took 1st and 2nd positions in the regional level among 450 Schools in Onitsha Archdiocese. In recent moral education championship examination set for primary 3, our pupils emerged 1st and 2nd for Basic3. Not only the intellectual aspect of human life does the schools care for, the school also care for spiritual aspect of our pupil’s life , because we strive for a holistic training of the pupils. The pupils are taught catechism and are guided to receive Holy Communion before passing out. So far, the schools have metamorphosed into a great school known by their products and changes in their past and present pupils which has brought so much honour to the schools, and still have greater things to offer the pupils and the society at large.

 


Francis Cardinal Arinze
