Our Facilities

Classrooms

Air conditioned/heated classrooms with networked computers and interactive whiteboards.

ICT Centre

Equipped with state-of-the-art computers and gadgets, softwares, wireless internet all to aid the teaching of the students.

Music Room

Houses various kinds of musical instruments both contemporary and traditional, including percussion, string and wind instruments.

Football Pitch

An outdoor field for football activities during playtimes and competitions.

Basketball Court

An outdoor field for basketball activities during playtimes and competitions.

Library

Spacious, air-conditioned room, stocked with relevant textbooks on the Nigerian and British curricula; journals, reference books, encyclopedias and novels.

Multi-Purpose Hall

Fully equipped multi-purpose hall with sound room, stage, multi media projector, toilet facilities e.t.c.

Science Laboratory

Fully equipped and well-stocked with state-of-the-art equipment and materials for conducting experiments and testing theories.

Art Room

Well-equipped art room, tailored to promote the discovery of students’ artistic talents and encourage creativity.

Boarding Facilities

Warm and inviting with all facilities needs, while at the same time, instills discipline.

Vocational/Skill Acquisition Centre

Facilities to help teaching and learning and also influence concrete and permanent change in technical behaviour.

Adventure Playgrounds

A fun, safe playground environment to stimulates students' minds and allows for their cognitive, physical, and social development.
About CDMIS

Our school is co-educational and multi-cultural, admitting students from within and outside Nigeria. A world class citadel of learning with a reputation for excellence
